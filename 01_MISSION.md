PROJECT MISSION

We are building a BTC/USDT automated trading research system.

The primary objective is NOT to trade immediately.

The primary objective is to determine whether a statistically valid trading edge exists using structured research, backtesting, and validation.

No live trading is allowed until full validation criteria are satisfied.

System must prioritize:
- Evidence over assumptions
- Validation over speed
- Risk control over profit expectations

Final goal:
Only deploy automation if a reproducible, statistically proven edge is confirmed.
