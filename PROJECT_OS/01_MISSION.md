ALPHAMOBILE QUANT OS — MISSION

PURPOSE

This document defines the permanent mission of the project.

The mission may not change without explicit review and approval.

────────────────────────────

CORE MISSION

Build a statistically validated, risk-controlled, fully auditable BTC/USDT trading research and automation system using only free-tier infrastructure and a mobile-first workflow.

The objective is NOT to automate trading.

The objective is NOT to maximize trade frequency.

The objective is NOT to maximize short-term profit.

The objective is:

To determine whether a genuine, repeatable, statistically defensible trading edge exists.

────────────────────────────

SUCCESS DEFINITION

Success is defined as:

1. A hypothesis is validated.
2. Validation criteria are satisfied.
3. Risk controls remain effective.
4. Results remain reproducible.
5. Automation behaves deterministically.

Profit alone does not constitute success.

────────────────────────────

CORE PRINCIPLES

Evidence over opinion.

Validation over excitement.

Research over assumptions.

Risk control over profit.

Consistency over complexity.

System integrity over speed.

────────────────────────────

ANTI-GOAL LIST

The system exists to avoid:

- gambling
- discretionary trading
- emotional decision making
- unvalidated optimization
- curve fitting
- undocumented changes
- architecture drift

────────────────────────────

FINAL PRINCIPLE

The purpose of AlphaMobile Quant OS is not to prove a strategy works.

The purpose is to discover the truth about whether a strategy works.

END OF DOCUMENT
