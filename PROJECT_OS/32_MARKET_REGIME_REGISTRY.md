ALPHAMOBILE QUANT OS — MARKET REGIME REGISTRY

PURPOSE

Provide a structured framework for evaluating strategy performance across different market environments.

No strategy may be considered validated without regime-level analysis.

────────────────────────────

CORE PRINCIPLE

A strategy that performs well in one regime and fails in another is not robust.

Performance must be measured by regime.

────────────────────────────

REGIME CATEGORIES

Higher Lows
Positive Momentum
Strong Trend Persistence

────────────────────────────

REGIME-002

Characteristics

────────────────────────────

REGIME-005

LOW VOLATILITY

────────────────────────────

────────────────────────────

REGIME-007

BLACK SWAN

Characteristics

────────────────────────────

RESEARCH RULES

Rule 1

Every backtest must identify regime.

Rule 2

Every paper trade must identify regime.

Rule 3

Every live trade must identify regime.

Rule 4

Strategy performance must be reported by regime.

────────────────────────────

VALIDATION RULES

A strategy is not considered robust unless:

Positive Expectancy exists across multiple regimes.

No single regime contributes more than 60% of total profitability.
